The Gemini AI app bridges the gap between your smart home and advanced artificial intelligence of Google, allowing you to control your home using natural language. Thanks to the Homey Dashboard Widget, you can chat directly with Gemini to ask questions about your home's status, control devices without rigid commands, or naturally schedule tasks. With the complete management of both standard and Advanced Flows, Gemini can also design, create, connect, and update complex logical automation paths with sequential actions, delays, and logical branching.

In addition to direct chat, Gemini acts as a powerful assistant within your Flows. You can use it to analyze images from your security cameras, process incoming messages to create a custom smart home bot, or generate intelligent voice announcements based on sensor triggers. Whether you need a quick answer or want to give your home the ability to dynamically evaluate situations before acting, this integration makes your daily life easier and your house truly smart.
